About Us

Keeley and Kevin both grew up in the suburbs of Rochester, NY. While they did not know each other growing up, they did meet at Kevin's college - SUNY Geneseo - through a mutual friend. They now both live in Boston, MA and, a year and a half ago, got enganged atop the Blue Hills Skyline hiking trail overlooking the Boston city skyline.

Keeley and Kevin love the outdoors. They clearly love hiking, but they also love camping and biking. They even spend as much time outside as possible in the winter months - skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, etc.

They spent the summer of 2016 traveling to areas nearby where they could camp and bike and loved every minute of it. When the academic year started up again following summer break, they were especially bummed. However, they both look very much forward to the travels they'll experience this upcoming summer!

List of places Keeley and Kevin traveled to in summer 2016:

  • Old Orchard Beach, ME
  • Canandaigua, NY
  • Martha's Vineyard, Cape Cod, MA
  • Newport, RI
  • Falmouth, MA
